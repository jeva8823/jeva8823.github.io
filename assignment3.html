Gouda Cheese

Creamy, sweet, yellow cow's milk cheese. Originated from the Netherlands in South Holland. Take a bite into the semi-hard to hard texture of gouda cheese.

History

Gouda cheese was first mentioned back in 1184. In South Holland, the cheese got its name after the master of Gouda. Gouda was traded in the city of South Holland as it was served as a delicacy. It is known as one of the oldest cheeses in the world. In the Dutch culture, cheese making was taken on by farmers' wives to pass down the tradition on to their daughters.
